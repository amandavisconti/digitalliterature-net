Things to Explore

  • On your Omeka.net site:
    • update your site settings (upper-right corner: “Settings” button)
    • activate and try out other themes and plugins
  • Read about all the Omeka plugins and themes (i.e. inlcuding those available only on Omeka.org sites) here (e.g. Image Annotation plugin)
  • Play around with the Scripto demo site
  • Browse the Omeka documentation, e.g. the page of things you should think about when planning an Omeka site
  • Check out the Omeka site showcase as well as the wiki list of Omeka sites
  • Add some different types of media (e.g. an mp3) to your Omeka.net site (see list of sources for finding more usable media below)

Find more public domain, CC-licensed, and/or reusable media at:

  • Creative Commons
  • Wikimedia Commons
  • LOC Flickr Stream
  • Samples of TEI texts (for testing your TEI plug-ins while you're still encoding your own texts)
